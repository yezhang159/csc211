How do I apply?

  1. Submit a CUNY application
    1. Create a portal account or log in to an existing account
    2. Choose the correct application for yourself (freshman or transfer)
  2. Submit payment for the corresponding application fee
    1. Freshman= $65
    2. Transfer= $70
  3. Submit All required documents for admissions purposes

What documents do I need?

  1. Freshman applicants must submit a proof of high school graduation in one of three ways:
    1. Official HS transcript in sealed envelope
    2. Original HS diploma
    3. Original GED Certificate
  2. Transfer applicants must submit the above as well as:
    1. Original Degree Certificate
    2. Offcial copy of your college transcript(s) from any/all college(s) attended
