Why BMCC?

  • Savings: Nine out of 10 BMCC graduates earn their degree, debt free.
  • Access: Convenient location in lower Manhattan and 9 sites throughout NYC.
  • Advancement: Variety of majors that prepare you for tomorrow's job market.
  • Experience: Hands-on learning from experts in various industries.
  • Transferable credits: Earn credits that can easily transfer to a bachelor's degree program.
  • Support: Team of faculty, staff and students to help you along the way.
  • Opportunity: Flexible classes; learning in the classroom and throughout the city.
